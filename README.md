# The Matrix Cave

> _A black monolith in a white void. One fire behind you. No explanation._

## The scene

A pure, overexposed white void. No horizon. No context.

At the center: a towering black cuboid — perfectly geometric, matte stone-grain texture, strictly monolithic.

At its base: a few scattered rocks and sparse dead grass tufts, the only concession to reality.

Behind you: a single point light flickering at the color temperature of fire (~1800K). It casts one shadow. The shadow is yours.

## Why

The allegory of the cave ends when the prisoner turns around and sees the fire. This is that moment — frozen. The monolith is not the shadow. The monolith is not the fire. The monolith is the thing that was always there, before either.
